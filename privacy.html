<h1>Hamlytics Privacy Policy</h1>


<p><strong>Last updated:</strong> January 2026</p>


<p>

Hamlytics is a Chrome extension that analyzes publicly available TikTok profile information

to provide analytics such as video counts and view statistics.

</p>


<h2>Information We Collect</h2>


<p>Hamlytics does <strong>not</strong> collect, store, or transmit:</p>

<ul>

  <li>Personally identifiable information</li>

  <li>Authentication credentials or passwords</li>

  <li>Financial or payment information</li>

  <li>Health data</li>

  <li>Personal communications</li>

  <li>Location data</li>

</ul>


<p>

The extension processes <strong>publicly available website content</strong> (such as TikTok profile

pages you actively visit) locally in your browser.

</p>


<h2>Pro Tokens</h2>


<p>

If you choose to enter a Pro token, it is stored locally using Chrome’s extension storage

and is used only to determine feature access. Tokens are never shared with third parties.

</p>


<h2>Data Usage</h2>


<p>

All analysis occurs locally within the browser or through Hamlytics services initiated

by the user. No user data is sold or shared.

</p>


<h2>Third Parties</h2>


<p>

Hamlytics does not sell or share user data with third parties.

</p>


<h2>Changes</h2>


<p>

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

</p>


<h2>Contact</h2>


<p>

If you have questions about this policy, please contact the Hamlytics team via the Chrome Web Store listing.

</p>
